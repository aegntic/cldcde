## Executive Summary

Prologue is positioned for exponential growth through a multi-faceted strategy that leverages its technological advantages, market timing, and ecosystem positioning. This pathway outlines a 5-year growth trajectory from initial launch to market leadership, with specific milestones, resource requirements, and strategic pivots.

**Growth Trajectory:**
- **Year 1**: Foundation - 50K users, $2.1M ARR
- **Year 2**: Expansion - 180K users, $8.4M ARR
- **Year 3**: Scale - 420K users, $21.6M ARR
- **Year 4**: Dominance - 1M users, $50M ARR
- **Year 5**: Leadership - 2.5M users, $120M ARR

**Total Addressable Opportunity: $4.2B global market**
**5-Year Target: 10x revenue growth, 20x user growth**

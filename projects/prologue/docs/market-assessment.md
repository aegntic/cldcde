## Executive Summary

Prologue is positioned at the intersection of two rapidly growing markets: AI development tools and Model Context Protocol (MCP) ecosystem management. With the projected MCP market reaching $2.8B by 2027 and AI development tools growing at 35% CAGR, Prologue addresses a critical pain point in the developer workflow.

**Key Findings:**
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: $4.2B globally
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)**: $1.8B (AI developers using MCP)
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: $420M (3-year target)
- **Growth Potential**: 10x revenue growth within 24 months
- **Competitive Advantage**: Universal platform compatibility + intelligent auto-discovery
